A
O2 is physisorbed
B

C
Occupancy of $$\pi _{2p}^*$$ of O2 is increased
D

Correct Option
C

Source
Chemistry • surface-chemistry
Explanation

Since, adsorption involves electron transfer from metal to O2, it is chemical adsorption not physical adsorption, hence (a) is incorrect. Also increase in occupancy of $$\pi$$*2p orbitals will decrease bond order and hence increase bond length of O2.
